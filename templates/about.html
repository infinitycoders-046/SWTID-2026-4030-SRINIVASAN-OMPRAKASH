⚡ Plugging into the Future

An Exploration of Electricity Consumption Patterns

This project explores electricity consumption patterns using advanced data analytics, visualization techniques, and predictive modeling.

The objective is to identify trends in energy consumption, compare usage across regions and sectors, and generate insights that support efficient energy management and future planning.
