Plugging into the Future: An Exploration of Electricity Consumption Patterns

The Story Behind Electricity Consumption

Electricity plays a vital role in modern society. As populations grow and industries expand, energy demand continues to rise.

Through our analysis, we discovered key consumption patterns across different sectors and time periods. Seasonal changes and economic activities significantly influence electricity demand.

Forecasting future energy needs can help governments, industries, and utility providers improve energy efficiency and sustainability planning.

This project highlights the importance of data-driven decision making in building a smarter and greener future.
